Cripsy Cheese Scrambled Eggs

Tired of plain ol' scrambled eggs? Love cripsy fried cheese? Spice up your breakfast with this scrambled eggs recipe

Ingredients

  • 2-4 eggs
  • 50-100 grams of cheddar cheese
  • Salt (To taste)
  • Pepper (pinch)
  • Cayanne Pepper (2 pinches)
  • Turmeric (pinch)
  • Cream (2 table spoons)

Use 2 eggs and 50 grams of cheese per person. Use more cheese if desired!

Instructions (hint: Click on the instruction to highlight your step)

  1. Once your non-stick frying pan is warm (low-medium heat), add in your cheese. Spread cheese out and let it thoroughly melt. Worried about the cheese sticking to your pan? Don't worry. The natural oils in the cheese will be released once the cheese starts melting
  2. While the cheese is melting, crack the eggs in a bowl and add the remaining ingredients to taste. Don't know what turmeric is? Click here here to discover all the health benefits of this yellow magic spice.
  3. Beat eggs, spices and cream with whisk thoroughly!
  4. Once cheese is thoroughly melted and has started to bubble, poor eggs over top cheese and increase head to medium
  5. lightly stir eggs while being careful not to scrape up the cheese. You want to leave the cheese stuck to the bottom of the pan
  6. Allow eggs to slightly cook before scrambling them carefully, don't scrape up the cheese. Continue this until eggs are very lightly cooked.
  7. Carefully wedge spatula underneath cheese lifting it from the pan entirely. Work your way around in a circle until it lifts off. If parts break, don't worry.
  8. Once cheese and eggs are completely unstuck from the pan, flip eggs over so the melted crispy cheese is on the top. If you're daring you can try flipping the eggs like a pro as seen in the gif below
  9. Let cook for less than 10 seconds and you're ready to serve!'
